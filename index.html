Porcupine-Run

Escape two porcupines, dodge their spikes, and lure them to flowers so they stop to eat. Score points, survive, and clear every level up to 20,000.

Desktop: Arrow keys
Mobile: Touch joystick
Flowers: +100 and distract porcupines for 3 seconds
Survival: +50 every 20 seconds
Created by Parth Pankaj Bakhda

How To Play

  • You have 8 hearts.
  • Two porcupines chase you and shoot spikes.
  • If a porcupine or spike hits you, you lose a heart.
  • Touch flowers to distract both porcupines for 3 seconds.
  • Each flower distraction gives 100 points.
  • Every 20 seconds survived gives 50 points.
  • Goals: 1000 → 2500 → 5000 → 7500 → 10000 → 12500 → 15000 → 17500 → 20000
