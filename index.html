Porcupine-Run

You are a civilian on a huge grass map. Two porcupines hunt you and shoot spikes. Dodge, survive, and lure porcupines to flowers to distract them.

Move: Arrow keys
Flowers: Touch to trigger porcupine eating (5s) +100 pts
Survival: +50 pts every 20s
Win: Reach each goal up to 20,000
Created by Parth Pankaj Bakhda

How to Play

  • Run from the porcupines. If they get close, they’ll hit you and you lose hearts.
  • They also shoot spikes. Dodge by zigzagging or moving up/down quickly.
  • Touch flowers to “activate” them: porcupines get distracted & eat for 5 seconds.
  • Each flower distraction gives you +100 points.
  • Every 20 seconds you survive: +50 points.
  • Reach the score goal to level up. Final goal is 20,000.
